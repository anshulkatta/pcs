Fix Calendar Syncing Issues

If you are experiencing syncing issues, such as duplicate tasks in Google Calendar, this will resolve the issues.

This process will:

  1. Deactivate calendar integration and delete the existing GQueues calendar and all events on that calendar.

  2. Reactivate calendar integration - creating a new GQueues calendar with a fresh copy of all of your tasks - and start syncing again.

  3. Take between 1 and 10 minutes depending on how many task are in your account.
